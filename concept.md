CAMPAIGN CONCEPT: SPORTS & ACTIVE LIFESTYLE

The Lunchbags bag is part of an active person's
daily kit — as natural as their racket, their
trainers, or their water bottle. This sprint
captures three distinct moments within a
sports-active day.

SET 1: OUTDOOR TENNIS COURT
Location: A classic outdoor tennis court with
clay or hard surface, white lines, natural
daylight. Bright, directional sunlight casting
hard shadows. Energy: focused, athletic, outdoors.
Props: tennis rackets, balls, water bottles,
towels, sunglasses.

SET 2: OUTDOOR BASKETBALL COURT
Location: An outdoor urban basketball court
with a painted concrete surface, metal hoops,
and chain-link fencing in the background.
Natural daylight, slightly overcast or golden
hour light. Energy: urban, casual, social.
Props: basketball, water bottles, backpacks,
sports towels, sneakers on the ground.

SET 3: OUTDOOR RUNNING / PARK TRAINING
Location: A park or outdoor fitness area —
grass, gravel path, or outdoor gym equipment.
Warm natural light, trees or greenery in the
background. Energy: fresh, active, open air.
Props: resistance bands, water bottles, running
shoes, earphones, food containers being unpacked
after a run.

VISUAL DIRECTION:
- Model is athletic, casual, real — not
  posed or overly styled
- Food in UNPACK shots: Mediterranean —
  fresh fruit, wraps, salads, yoghurt
- Bag is always the natural hero — never
  forced or artificially prominent
- Each set should feel like a different
  chapter of the same active day

REFERENCE IMAGES:
references/Set1/ — outdoor tennis court scenes,
  tennis players, rackets, balls, court surfaces
references/Set2/ — outdoor basketball court,
  urban courts, players, hoops, concrete
references/Set3/ — park running, outdoor
  training, greenery, fitness people outdoors

Each set folder should contain 3-6 images
that define the visual world of that set.

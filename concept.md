# Campaign Concept

The concept is a sunny afternoon on a tennis court. The lunch bag belongs to someone who plays tennis or padel — it sits on the court surface, next to a racket, tennis balls, and a cold drink. The setting is always the court — green or blue hard surface, white lines visible, net in the background occasionally.
The mood is sporty, confident, and effortlessly cool. Strong overhead sun creating hard shadows on the court surface. The colour story is green, yellow, and white — the natural palette of a tennis court.
No full-body model shots. Only legs, hands, and partial body present — cropped, casual, intimate. The focus is always the product and the court props around it. The bag is part of the athlete's kit — as natural on a tennis court as the racket and the balls.
Props vocabulary: vintage and modern tennis rackets, tennis and padel balls, cold drinks in cans or cups, sport socks and sneakers visible at the edges of frame, court lines and net.
The light is hard midday sun — sharp shadows, high contrast, warm but not golden. Everything feels like it was shot between sets on a real court, not in a studio.
